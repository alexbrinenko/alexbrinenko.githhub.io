Agridera Fresh Market Pineapple Hybrids

××××××

Agridera aims to become one of the world's leading companies in pineapple breeding, production, and marketing. We actively invest in research and development to provide solutions to growers and consumers in many countries worldwide. In particular, we breed improved disease-resistant varieties adapted to diverse global markets. Our current focus is on developing resistance to common pineapple diseases such as heart rot, mealybug wilt, and black rot.

From a horticultural perspective, we aim to improve characteristics such as fruit size and shape, sweetness, aroma, shelf life, and resistance to internal browning. We target both round and cylindrical forms, focusing on uniformity, transportability, and consumer preference.
Agridera's pineapple breeding programs are focused on three main segments: Greenhouse Pineapples, Open-field Pineapples for Tropical Climates, and Pineapples for Mediterranean Climates.

Greenhouse Pineapple Breeding Program

Targeted at controlled environment agriculture. This program develops compact plants with fast fruit development, excellent sweetness, and aroma. Special attention is paid to resistance against mealybug wilt and fusarium.

Open-field Pineapples for Tropical Climates

This breeding is designed for markets in Brazil, Costa Rica, and the Philippines. We focus on large-sized fruits, uniform ripening, and tolerance to heart rot and internal browning. Shelf life and sweetness are also top priorities.

Pineapples for Mediterranean Climates

We develop round and cylindrical shapes with medium to large fruit sizes (from 1.2 to 2.5 kg). Emphasis is placed on aroma, sugar-acid balance, and storage quality. Resistance to black rot and fusarium is also incorporated.
Agridera operates through international and local distributors around the world.
