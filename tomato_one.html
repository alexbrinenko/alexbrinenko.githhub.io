Pineapple Hybrid
Golden Sweet Variety

World-renowned for its flavor, sweetness, and long shelf life.

Exceptionally sweet taste
with extended freshness!

CHARACTERISTICS
Resistance
Good resistance to internal browning and blackheart.
Tolerant to shipping and handling stress.

Plant

Compact plant structure with strong roots.
High productivity and uniform growth.
Suitable for tropical and subtropical climates.

Fruit

Cylindrical shape with golden-yellow skin.
Crisp texture and high Brix (sugar) level.
Average fruit weight: 1.2 – 1.5 kg.

Cycle

Year-round production
Harvest: 14–16 months after planting

Color

Golden Yellow
